We are TigerConnect, a leading healthcare technology company dedicated to transforming the way clinical teams communicate and collaborate. Our mission is to unify communications both inside and outside hospitals so that healthcare professionals—from doctors and nurses to patients and administrative staff—stay securely and efficiently connected. The TigerConnect platform is used by healthcare organizations across the United States, processing millions of messages daily with the highest standards of security, reliability, and availability.

We develop a comprehensive clinical communication and collaboration platform that enables care teams to share real-time information through secure text, voice, video, and alerts all in strict compliance with HIPAA and HITRUST standards. Our solutions encompass clinical communication, physician scheduling, alarm management, patient engagement, workflow automation, and pre-hospital coordination. Everything is designed to accelerate processes, enhance patient safety, and optimize clinical outcomes.

At TigerConnect, we believe that technological innovation drives real improvements in people's lives. Working here means being part of a multidisciplinary team passionate about solving complex healthcare challenges through high-impact software. We offer a collaborative environment where creativity and critical thinking are essential, alongside opportunities for professional and technical growth. By developing solutions that millions of users rely on daily to communicate and save lives, our engineering teams build products that truly matter, fostering a deep sense of purpose, belonging, and professional pride.
