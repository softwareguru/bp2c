### Why aren't there more companies?
We know that some people consider other companies good options to work for but they are not on this list. Here are some reasons:

* Not all of them decide to take the test.
* Possibly they have their own mechanisms.
* Are enrolled in other programs or awards (although Best Place To Code is different from them)
* They were evaluated but decided not to earn Best Place To Code membership.
* They are simply unaware of this type of accreditation.

### About Best Place To Code

It is an initiative of Software Guru to recognize the companies in which we recommend those who work in the areas of software and information technology to collaborate. In other words, an organization where it is a pleasure to work as an information technology professional, regardless of the company's size, location, or industry.

First of all, "Best place to code" is a seal of quality. It is a registered trademark of Software Guru, which accredits organizations that prove to be an attractive option for software developers.
